What is a Vision Board?

"A vision board is a collage of images, words, and affirmations that represent a person's goals, dreams, and aspirations. It serves as a visual tool for motivation and inspiration, helping to keep your intentions top of mind and focus your actions. Vision boards can be physical, made on a poster board or in a notebook, or digital."
